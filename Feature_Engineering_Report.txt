FEATURE ENGINEERING REPORT
-----------------------------------
1. income_to_loan_ratio – Measures repayment capacity.
2. high_dti – Flags borrowers with high debt-to-income ratio.
3. long_credit_history – Indicates credit history > 10 years.
4. many_open_accounts – Detects borrowers with > 8 accounts.
5. high_revol_util – Shows heavy revolving credit utilization.
